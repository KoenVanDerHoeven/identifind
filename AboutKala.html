About KALA:

We are a group of University of Pennsylvania engineers interested in app development. This is our second PennApps as a group. If you would like to contact us, we conveniently all live in the same building. KALA Studios are located at Kings Court 250, or 200, but that’s a little far away. We’re just a fun-loving group of hackers.
